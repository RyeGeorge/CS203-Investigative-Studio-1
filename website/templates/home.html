The Shotgun

The pirate's shotgun delivers devastating blows with its wide spray pattern, obliterating all enemies in your way.

The shotgun doubles as a movement tool, launching the player through the air when fired during jumps, enabling swift traversal.

The Movement

The pirate skillfully slides down vertical walls, navigating hazards. A timed jump off walls grants acrobatic escapes and access to higher platforms.

The pirate swiftly dashes through the island, evading enemies and obstacles with lightning-fast movements and unmatched agility.

The Enemies

The formidable crab scuttles across the island, its pincers poised to snap and its armored shell providing formidable defense against attacks.

The relentless starfish spins with dazzling speed, launching itself towards the player, its spiky arms ready to strike and impede their progress.

The clam, nestled within its protective shell, skillfully launches projectile pearls at the player, unleashing a barrage of dangerous projectiles to hinder their advancement.
